Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Food Hub

How do I place an order? ‚ñº
Simply log in to your account, browse the available food options, select your items, and complete your order. You'll receive a confirmation and estimated pickup time.
How does the queue system work? ‚ñº
When you place an order, you're added to a virtual queue. The system calculates your estimated wait time based on current orders and notifies you when your food is ready for pickup.
What payment methods are accepted? ‚ñº
We accept cash, credit/debit cards, and digital wallets. Some food outlets may have specific payment preferences, which will be indicated during checkout.
Can I cancel or modify my order? ‚ñº
You can modify or cancel your order within 5 minutes of placing it. After that, the order enters preparation and cannot be changed.
What if I miss my pickup time? ‚ñº
If you miss your pickup window, your order will be held for up to 30 minutes. After that, it may be discarded, and you may be charged a small fee for wasted food.
Is there a minimum order amount? ‚ñº
Most food outlets have a minimum order amount of ‚Ç±50. This helps ensure efficient operations and timely service for all students.
